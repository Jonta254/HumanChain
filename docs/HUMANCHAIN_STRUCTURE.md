# HumanChain Product Structure

## Identity

HumanChain is a mobile-first World Mini App for verified humans.

Tagline:

> Ask the world. Follow the thread.

Core promise:

> Ask real humans. Get the world's verdict.

## Design Style

HumanChain uses an `Atlas Paper + Living Threads` style:

- Warm paper base.
- Deep ink typography.
- Blue for Ask The World.
- Gold for WLD value and Human Verdicts.
- Coral for Daily Chain participation.
- Green for Human Story and growth.
- Thin connected thread visuals for chains and answers.

## Main Tabs

### Home

Purpose: make the app feel alive immediately.

Includes:

- HumanChain hero.
- Live global activity strip.
- Ask The World quick action.
- Join Today's Chain quick action.
- Read Human Story quick action.
- Human Streak card.
- Trending Human Verdict.
- Human Pulse.

### Ask

Purpose: the main winning feature.

Flow:

1. User writes a real question.
2. Chooses topic.
3. Chooses free or WLD-powered reach.
4. Publishes.
5. Verified humans answer.
6. Human Verdict forms.

Question categories:

- Love
- Money
- Business
- Family
- Culture
- Faith
- Loneliness
- Moving abroad
- AI vs Human
- Life decisions

### Chains

Purpose: the daily habit.

Each day has one global prompt. Every verified human can add one link:

- text
- image
- voice
- advice
- confession
- local phrase
- memory

### Stories

Purpose: monthly emotional depth.

Human Story is a page-based monthly story about being human.

Monthly themes:

- January: Starting Over
- February: Love and Loss
- March: Money and Survival
- April: Family
- May: Dreams
- June: Loneliness
- July: Migration
- August: Forgiveness
- September: Youth
- October: Faith
- November: Regret
- December: Hope

### Me

Purpose: personal identity and retention.

Includes:

- Human Passport
- Human Streak
- badges
- questions asked
- answers given
- links added
- countries connected
- saved verdicts
- saved stories
- WLD activity

## WLD Feature Ladder

The app uses small WLD payments for value, visibility, and deeper perspective.

| Price | Feature | User Benefit |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 WLD | Tip, Golden Link, Streak Restore | Thank a human, highlight a link, or restore a streak once per month |
| 2 WLD | Pin Link, Story Bonus Pages | More visibility or more story depth |
| 3 WLD | Ask One Country, Memory Capsule | Get country-specific answers or save a special link |
| 4 WLD | Private Verified Ask | Ask anonymously while proving humanity |
| 5 WLD | Voice Answers | Receive short voice replies from verified humans |
| 6 WLD | Deep Human Verdict or Deep Human Mirror | Unlock country differences, best answers, and a full summary |

## World-Native Tools

### Wallet Auth

Use for sign-in and account creation.

### IDKit Proof of Human

Use for proof-of-human actions:

- one Daily Chain link per day
- one vote per Human Verdict
- one answer per question when needed
- anonymous but verified posts

### Pay

Use for all WLD purchases. Always verify payments on the backend.

### Notifications

Use for:

- Human Verdict ready
- new answers
- tip received
- streak ending today
- monthly Human Story published

### Microphone

Use for:

- Voice of Humanity
- voice answers
- story reflections

### Share Contacts

Use for:

- inviting contacts to answer a question
- inviting contacts to a chain
- sharing monthly Human Story

### Haptics

Use for:

- payment success
- answer posted
- link added
- streak kept
- verdict unlocked

## MVP Launch Order

1. Build static UI shell.
2. Add World Wallet Auth.
3. Add question and answer storage.
4. Add Daily Chain storage.
5. Add payment reference and payment verification.
6. Add Human Streak calculation.
7. Add Monthly Human Story content model.
8. Test inside World App.
9. Submit to Mini App Store.
